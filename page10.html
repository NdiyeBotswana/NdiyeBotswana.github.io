Do you have what it takes to be a diamond?

The journey of a diamondteur

The journey of being an entrepreneur in the diamond industry has been the most satisfying but challenging journey thus far. When I began I didn't believe I had the necessary minerals to be an entrepreneur due to the large amount of pressure around. It is at such moments you need to ask yourself what you want from life. Most journeys end when the pressure becomes too much to handle but as you’ll learn on Clarity Bw, diamonds are formed under pressure. 

Living in an economy where jobs are scarce, seeing your peers run towards secure jobs will probably be your biggest conflicting notion as an entrepreneur. It is important to understand that you’re comprised of different psychological minerals compared to other people. Just like the formation of a diamond, different carbonated minerals are required. Those different minerals with time will be placed under immense pressure.

Mobirise

I urge those with an entrepreneurial dream to trust what they have during good and bad times. If you keep working on your dreams on a daily basis your ideas will be able to withstand pressure and once the pressure is taken off, you’ll be a diamond. For diamonds are no different to us entrepreneurs. They go through years of dirt, pressure and confusion. It’s those very few minerals that stand firm in their place that become one of the world’s most valued jewels.

So I’ll end this article with a question. Do you have what it takes to become a diamond?
