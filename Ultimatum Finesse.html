Ultimatum Finesse

From Diamonds to The Tony Elumelu Foundation.

Kabo Makuke is Botswana's very own Elon Musk in the making. With his mind focused on innovation and his heart set on growing communities, we can deduce that Ultimatum Finesse should be a company to look out for in the future. 

Writer: Kabo Makuke
Position: Ultimatum Finesse founder and Managing Director

Not every super hero wears a cape and flies over the world. Some heroes fly drones, take pictures and make sure where you wish to live or work would have a well-planned and designed structure. I’m the Founder of Ultimatum Finesse which is the only source you need for all your Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Geographic Positioning Systems (GPS), CAD, Mapping, Urban Planning and Drone based mapping.  

I realized everybody wants to be a hero and for me Ultimatum finesse would be my entrepreneurial cape. With an Entrepreneurial mind, I entered the Okavango Diamond Company Youth Entrepreneurship Programme (ODC YEP) with the vision of introducing drone technology to help with mapping for mines. 

The ODC programme created a shift in my thinking. The learning environment was different to the point where I saw a vision of Ultimatum Finesse go from being an ordinary GIS company to being a lead innovator within Botswana. The ODC programme taught me the most valuable entrepreneur lesson to date and that is “ To not let my mind limit my dreams but to let my dreams be limitless in my mind” 

The end of the ODC Programme was the beginning of my hunger to be part of different learning environments. In 2018 I decided to take Ultimatum Finesse on a learning journey. A journey that would change how my company and I would operate and look. This new journey is with The Tony Elumelu Foundation.
