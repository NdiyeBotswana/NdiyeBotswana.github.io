My ODC YEP Journey

An academic trying to shine.

Born in Orapa and raised in Kimberley. One could say diamonds have played a large role in my life. Once I graduated from Varisty, my dream was to always run my own research syndicate to help organizations and consumers communicate more effectively.

In 2017 I was given the unique opportunity to be part of the Okavango Diamond Company Youth Entrepreneurship Programme.

During this programme I was exposed to facets of the diamond industry that a very few get the opportunity to see. This programme educated 10 citizens about the industry and with the assistance of the University of Stellenbosch’s Business School, we were molded into having an entrepreneur mindset. 

It was through the ODC YEP where I developed the idea of creating Clarity to help Batswana get a better understanding of the diamond industry with the intentions of igniting the hearts of other Batswana to enter the industry. 

I’d like to thank Kutlo Thatana, Stellenbosch and ODC for the unique opportunity and to Batswana, I urge you to follow your passions for anything could happen with daily effort.
