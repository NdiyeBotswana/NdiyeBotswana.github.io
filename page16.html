DE BEERS NOW TRACKS DIAMONDS USING BLOCKCHAIN TECH

De Beers has launched a blockchain system which allows the companies to track diamonds from the mine to the retailer. This system was introduced to eradicate imposters and conflict stones in the markets. Since its inception, 100 diamonds have been tracked from mines to end users. 

De Beers has led the industry into using this system to ensure all gems are authentic. By creating a digital trail, De Beers can now track the stone through each stage and will also be able to identify accountable parties if any issues arise. 
Five diamond manufacturers worked with De Beers to develop the blockchain platform called “Tracr”. The involved manufacturers are Diacore, Diarough. KGK group, Rosy Blue NV and Venus Jewel. This platform will be launched to the rest of diamond industry at the end of the year.
