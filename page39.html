2019 Timeline

Summary of events in the Botswana diamond industry 

January 2019

TIFFANY & CO

1. Tiffany & Co have introduced sharing a diamond’s provenance (country of origin) with consumers. The “Diamond Source Initiative” was created to advance diamond transparency between customers and the industry.

January 2019  

Lucara Diamond Corp

2. Lucara Diamond Corp recovered a 127-ct white diamond from their Karowe mine.

February 2019

Botswana Diamonds

 Botswana Diamonds and Vast were granted permissionto Mine in Marange Zimbabwe.

February 2019

Botswana Diamonds

Botswana Diamonds hits Kimberlite at Thorny River in South Africa

March 2019

Botswana media 

Media speculates about Botswana handling the polishing of Zimbabwe’s diamonds.

18 March 2019

Cut 9 coming to Jwaneng Mine

Debswana announces Cut 9 for the Jwaneng mine to Extend the mines life.

March 2019

Botswana Diamonds starts production

John Teeling (Chairman of Botswana Diamonds) announced that Botswana Diamonds will be doing exploration and production at Thory River

17 April 2019

ODC unveils Botswana's largest Blue Diamond

State-run firm, Okavango Diamond Company, unveiled "The Okavango Blue" diamond in honor of the Okavango Delta

April 2019

Lucara recovers the second largest in history

Lucara Diamond Corp. discovered the second largest diamond (1758 carats) from it's Karowe Mine.

June 2019

Bostwana government aims to buy shares in Lucara Diamond Corp.

Presidfrny Mokgweetsi Masisi is said to have gone into negotions with Lucara Diamond Corp. about buying shares in Lucara so that the Government can invest in the company. 

June 2019

Gem Diamonds finally sells Ghaghoo Mine in Botswana

Gem Diamonds sold Ghaghoo to Pro Civil for R77m
