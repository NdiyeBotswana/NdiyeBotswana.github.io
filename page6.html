FAQs

What is Clarity

Clarity in general is a platform for the Botswana diamond industry to communicate with Batswana and the world more effectively.

 How does Clarity benefit the people of Botswana

Clarity's social responsibility is aimed at educating the people of Botswana about the diamond industry. The purpose of Clarity is to get the people of Botswana engaged in the industry to help find opportunities for themselves to enter.

 What jobs are available within the diamond industry

For recent updates on jobs you can visit our Careers page. We publish mining and diamond related jobs on the website and on our social media pages. We also offer people the opportunity to become freelance journalists for CLARITY.
