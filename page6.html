FAQs

What is Clairy

Clarity in general is an advertising platform for the Botswana diamond industry to communicate with Batswana and the world.

 How does Clarity benefit the people of Botswana

Clarity's social responsibility is aimed at educating the people of Botswana about the diamond industry. The purpose of Clarity is to get the people of Botswana engaged in the industry to help find opportunities for themselves to enter.

 What jobs are available within the diamond industry

For recent updates on jobs you can visit our Careers page. Clarity publishes diamond related jobs on the website and on it's social media sites. Clarity also offers people the opportunity to become journalist for our articles within the News section and also gives sales people the opportunity to become Jewelery store sales agents.
