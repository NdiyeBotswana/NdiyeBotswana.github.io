WELCOME TO THE DIAMOND INDUSTRY

Welcome and thank you for coming to Clarity Botswana. The diamond value chain is a long complex process that has been beneficial to the people of Botswana since its inception. Clarity Botswana will aim to provide relevant content about the Botswana Diamond Industry with the goal of educating the nation about Diamonds. The diamond value chain has 7 stages which will be examined in detail on this site.

Clarity Botswana will use it's Facebook page and twitter pages to get individuals to engage with the articles and to ask question which will be addressed within this platform.

We hope you enjoy and engage with us during this journey.
