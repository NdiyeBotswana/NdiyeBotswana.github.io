GOD'S  DIAMOND KEEPS SHINNING

From polishing to jewellery and building. Veronica is an entrepreneur aimming to employ and leave her mark in every industry she enters.

Author: Ndiye Ngubula  

Last year we wrote an article about Veronica Mosweu on how she entered the diamond industry and now we’re proud to announce her next step in journey. Veronica is the first Motswana to be offered a scholarship by World Gem Foundation to study Career Gemology.

This is a major step in the Botswana Gemology industry for there is no renowned career program for gemologists in Botswana. The World Gem Foundation is a non-profit organization that was formed in 2015 to create affordable gemological training to students, jewelers and professional gemologists. Gemology is the study of gemstone materials. As we know Botswana has the highest value gems stones and with a programme like this, we can find more effective ways of using our gemstones within Botswana. 

Veronica has been search for such an article for years and during Christmas she stumbled upon a scholarship that would not just change her life but will pave the way for other Batswana in the future. Some may say that it was luck that lead to her this opportunity, but she disclosed that she has been searching for such a scholarship for more than 5 years. Veronica describes her luck as a result of consistency and always being ready for what you dream of. 

Veronica’s passion for gemstones was discovered during her third year at the University of Botswana where she majored in Economics. During her third year she noticed an advert in a newspaper which offered people an opportunity to be trained as diamond manufacturers in Thailand Bangkok. Since that moment, she has conquered different battles and has stuck to the choice of breaking into the diamond industry.

Becoming an entrepreneur is a hard enough task on its own, but to find a break into an old industry such as the diamond industry is a lot harder. “ Despite all the criticism and negativity during the journey, I held unto this dream because I believe in it and my myself. So I want to encourage someone out there to fight for their dreams and passion. Do not be afraid to break patterns and traditions, just remember that you can be THE FIRST as well”.
