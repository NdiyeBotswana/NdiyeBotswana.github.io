Healthy Diamonds

MY ODC YEP JOURNEY

Mining and office work can have a heavy strain on the body over a period of time. As a physiotherapist my goal has been to improve the health-related quality of life of individuals. In August 2017 I received the opportunity to be part of the Okavango Diamond Company’s entrepreneurship programme.

During this journey I was exposed to various parts of the diamond industry which I could relate to. 

When it was time to create a business plan for my entrepreneurial project, I realized my vision of wellness for both employers and employees was vital in all the different area within the diamond value chain.

My project was aimed at providing physiotherapy and wellness services for individuals in the industry to improve their productivity. 

During my presentation, I remember looking at my colleagues and thinking that it was such an honour to be part of such programme filled with such amazing individuals. At the end of the journey, I received the award for coming 1st in programme. Coming first was a pleasure but the true victory was being part of something that could change the lives of so many vital people in Botswana.

I’d like to thank the Okavango diamond company Youth Entrepreneurship Programme for the amazing opportunity. I’d also like to thank my colleagues who were part of the programme for always supporting one another and hope that all our entrepreneur dreams come true.

Writer: Tito Chimbise
Position:  Physio Therapist and ODC YEP 1st Place

Tito Chimbise is an acting physio theropist at ( PLACE). She was one of the 10 candidates chosen to be part of the Okavango diamond company's Youth Entrepreneurship programme  in 2017 where she recieved the highest award.
