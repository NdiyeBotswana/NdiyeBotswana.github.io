TOP DIAMOND PRODUCING COUNTRIES 

There are two major bands where diamonds are found, the northern band and the southern band. There is a central band but the diamonds produced there are of a lower quantity and value. Diamond producing countries are countries where diamonds are mined and found. These countries fall into the three bands. 

Canada and Russia fall into the northern band, two of the leading diamond producing countries (in terms of quantity). The Southern band has Botswana, South Africa, Namibia and Australia, Botswana is the worldâ€™s leading diamond producer (In terms of value). The Central band has countries such as Ivory Coast, Sierra Leone, Venezuela and Brazil.

BOTSWANA

Botswana is known for its blue skies, amazing tourist destinations and amazing meat but it's also known as the land which produces the most valuable diamonds and has the largest deposit of Gem stones in the world. 

Botswana is the leading diamond producing country in the world (In terms of value and second in volume). It's also known as the home base of De Beers (Known as Debswana in Botswana). Orapa and Jwaneng are Botswana's most prolific mines aside from the 5 other mines.

RUSSIA

Russia  is believed to have the world's largest diamond resources. They are the largest producer and exporter of rough diamonds by volume. It is the home of ALROSA.

Diamonds mined in Russia come in all sizes, colors and clarities. They are known for their fluorescence. Fluorescence in diamonds is a less favourable trait for gem quality diamonds.

THE REPUBLIC OF CONGO

The Democratic Republic of Congo is Africa's Largest diamond producer. Due to the unsettled environment, details on production remain semi valid. Despite the political stance, the DRC is the third largest diamond-producing country by volume in the world.
