WHY WEAR GUCCI WHEN GUCCI WANTS TO WEAR SWANA?

Local jewelry designs 

Last week I had an eye opener experience while I roamed the city of Francistown. There I was in a mall looking at the famous brands and wondering what would the value of these items be in a couple of years. Just as I turned around a lady (who was probably in her mid 60s) asked me “Why are so many people drawn to these expensive temporary goods?”. At that moment I froze and said “the people here live on social media and television. Their understanding of these goods is linked to success and comfort”. 
She just laughed and said “I don’t understand why people in Botswana want to wear Gucci when people who actually wear Gucci want to wear Swana”. Sometimes it takes an outsider to help appreciate the true value we have within. In this case, a mature woman indicated that our Swana diamond jewelry design is of more financial and emotional value compared to the clothing brands we are fixated on. (If you don’t know what Swana is click on this link).

As Batswana, it may be important that we start to appreciate the products created within our borders. This applies to clothing, food, furniture and jewelry. Yes that Gucci belt will look good on you now but next season the value would have dropped but if you bought a Swana diamond it would be precious forever.
