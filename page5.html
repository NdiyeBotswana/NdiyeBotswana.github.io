Who are we?


Clarity Bw is a division of Diamond Value Investments (DVI). It is Botswana’s first interactive communication channel between the people of Botswana and the diamond industry. Clarity Bw’s creation was created to get the people of Botswana engaged and interested in activity in the diamond industry.
