Who are we


Clarity Bw is a division of Diamond Value Investment (DVI). It is Botswana’s first interactive communication channel between the people of Botswana and the diamond industry. Clarity Bw’s creation was created to get the people of Botswana engaged and interested in activity in the diamond industry. 

Ndiye Ngubula - CEO

Clarity Bw's creator and leader. He's a research analyst by profession but has made it his plan to better the lives of people within Botswana by educating them about the value of the diamond industry.
