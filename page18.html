DIAMOND EXPLORATION IN BOTSWANA

Diamond discoveries come in various forms around the world. In Botswana diamonds are commonly found in Kimberlites. Kimberlite is a type of potassic volcanic rock where crystalized diamonds are discovered.

Different types of exploration

Airborne Geophysics

Airborne Geophysics is the process of exploring the land using a plane or drone. Used as a reconnaissance tool. This method allows exploration teams to covers a large area in a short time (usually months).

Can be aeromagnetics or gravity technique. Line spacing and flight height can be varied
